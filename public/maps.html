Competitive Maps

In Valorant, there are several maps in the game. Here are all the maps below. You can favorite maps by clicking the ☆ button on each card, and add personal notes!

Ascent

Open mid area with two bomb sites. Classic competitive map with tight chokepoints and long sightlines.

Bind

No mid area, two sites connected by teleporters. Fast rotations and unique angles.

Haven

Three bomb sites! Requires versatile strategies and good map control across multiple lanes.

Split

Vertical map with ropes and elevated areas. Close-quarters combat and quick peeks.

Lotus

Three-site map with rotating doors and a destructible wall. Ancient ruins theme.

Sunset

Urban Los Angeles setting. Multiple elevation changes and aggressive playstyle opportunities.

Icebox

Arctic map with vertical ziplines and containers. Long sightlines and unique plant spots.

Breeze

Tropical paradise with wide open spaces. Long-range engagements dominate here.

Fracture

H-shaped map with defenders split. Unique ziplines and aggressive attack routes.

Pearl

Underwater Omega Earth city. Classic layout with focus on mid control.

Abyss

Floating castle map with death pits. High-risk high-reward plays near edges.

Corrode

Industrial decay map with toxic elements. Experimental layout for tactical plays.
